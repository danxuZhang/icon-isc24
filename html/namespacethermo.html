Calculates the internal energy of a system.

This function calculates the internal energy of a system based on the temperature, specific mass of water vapor, liquid phases, solid phases, density, and layer thickness. The internal energy is computed using the moist isometric specific heat and latent heats of vaporization and sublimation.

Parameters
[in]tTemperature (K).
[in]qvSpecific mass of water vapor (kg/kg).
[in]qliqSpecific mass of liquid phases (kg/kg).
[in]qiceSpecific mass of solid phases (kg/kg).
[in]rhoDensity (kg/m^3).
[in]dzLayer thickness (m).
Returns
The internal energy of the system (J/m^2).

Calculates the temperature from the internal energy of a system.

This function calculates the temperature of a system based on the internal energy, specific mass of water vapor, liquid phases, solid phases, density, and layer thickness. The temperature is computed by solving the internal energy equation for temperature, considering the moist isometric specific heat and latent heats of vaporization and sublimation.

Parameters
[in]UInternal energy of the system (J/m^2).
[in]qvSpecific mass of water vapor (kg/kg).
[in]qliqSpecific mass of liquid phases (kg/kg).
[in]qiceSpecific mass of solid phases (kg/kg).
[in]rhoDensity (kg/m^3).
[in]dzLayer thickness (m).
Returns
The temperature of the system (K).
